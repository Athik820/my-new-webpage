About ICST University

A premier institution dedicated to excellence in science, technology, and innovation.

ICST University Park is a leading institution in science and technology education, established in 2005. With a focus on innovation, research, and hands on learning, ICST offers top tier undergraduate and postgraduate programs in Computer Science, IT, Electronics, and AI. The university provides modern facilities, experienced faculty, and strong industry partnerships, helping students succeed in both academics and careers. Located in a peaceful, tech friendly environment, ICST University Park is the ideal destination for future innovators and leaders.
